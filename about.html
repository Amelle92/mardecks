About Mardeck's

Mardeck's has been restoring beauty to homes, like yours, since 1994. Whether you're in need for a minor touch-up, major restoration, or a new installation, our experts will advise you with honesty and integrity.

We are experts in cleaning and restoring all types of exterior surfaces including wood decks, hardwood flooring, siding, patios, concrete, walkways, and gazebo restoration.

We provide a premium service for a reasonable price. We have been servicing the greater DC Metropolitan area for more than 15 years and have many local references to share with you.

We are a family business, and believe in satisfying our customers 100% of the time. Guaranteed! So please, give us a call, or send us a line, you will not be disappointed!
